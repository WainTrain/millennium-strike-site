Airing Since
April 25th, 2021

Millennium Strike is a bi-monthly show hosted on Twitch.com with each episode featuring audio / video performances and DJ sets. Each episode includes 10-minute intermission lobbies to take advantage of the show’s atmosphere and further engage the audience with retro and aesthetically pleasing commercials. The goal is to make each performance feel like a TV program from the 90’s – 00’s.

These concepts were originally folded into the “SYNESTHESIA” URL event series at Eternal Virtual Ascension (EVA) until that collective’s fall in mid-2021.

Millennium Strike would officially begin its run on April 25th, 2021, founded by Ashton Simeon with the support of many friends from past online communities.

Millennium Strike is co-lead by Ashton Simeon and taffa.

History

2019
4FTERLIFE_0NLINE

The initial beginnings of our group started in 2019 with the online community 4FTERLIFE_0NLINE or 4F_0.

An online space dedicated to exploring a style referred to as “Neo-Rave”, a modern reemergence of early 2000’s music and visual feelings popularized at the time by Porter Robinson’s “Virtual Self” project.

The community released various compilations and hosted two alternating URL shows called NEUROTIC and OBLIVEON.

The basic DNA structure of how Millennium Strike operates comes from this era.

MS artists: Mailto, netsylph, Neir, arenagate, Foltik, DJR3, tenshi, MMIC, Ashton Simeon, ident, DAY21 etc. would all meet for the first time within this community.

2020
Eternal Virtual Ascension (EVA)

EVA was the eventual continuation of our group becoming more label focused with its many releases and thriving with its SYNESTHESIA URL shows.

SYNESTHESIA began with a similar format to 4FTERLIFE_0NLINE's shows, but later leaned into a more TV-inspired style with commercial breaks between performers.

EVA's initial visual identity was inspired by cyber brutalism, but would eventually become laxer and more inviting with its aesthetic as it went on.

MS artists: KIRA600, Key Archive, Zrmpa, Digital Reject, Ghostmemory, YAN, Tony Astro, Skill System, Xyther, taffa, MACHINEANGEL, etc. would make their first appearances & contributions here.

2021
Millennium Strike

Millennium Strike was conceptualized as far back as August 5th, 2020 as just a show on Twitch.com.

The narrative/identity of the show was pictured as a new programming block of internet rave content premiering on a fictional TV station called “Channel 805”, whose aesthetic was inspired by “The Weather Channel”.

The fictional programming block “Millennium Strike” would then contrast the aesthetic of Channel 805, being colorful and bombastic to mimic the feel of Saturday morning cartoon blocks such as “JETIX, FoxBox, 4Kids.tv, etc.”

In 2023, the MS lore was tweaked a bit.

“A defunct TV network: Channel 805 has been hijacked by the infamous group of rave TV pirates known as Millennium Strike.”

Millennium Strike wasn't originally conceptualized as a collective all the way back in 2020, but today it has gone so much farther than just being another Twitch show. MS is a wonderful community with some of the most talented DJs, producers, visual artists, as well as some of the most incredible friends.
